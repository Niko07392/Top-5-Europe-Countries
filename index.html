Top 5 European Countries

  1. Romania

    Romania is known for its beautiful Carpathian Mountains, medieval castles, and the region of Transylvania. It has a rich history, amazing landscapes, and friendly people.

  2. Spain

    Spain is famous for its sunny beaches, delicious food, football culture, and historic cities such as Madrid and Barcelona.

  3. France

    France is known for its art, fashion, cuisine, and famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower. It is one of the most visited countries in the world.

  4. Italy

    Italy is famous for pizza, pasta, ancient Roman history, and beautiful cities such as Rome, Venice, and Florence.

  5. Germany

    Germany is known for its strong economy, engineering, castles, and cultural traditions such as Oktoberfest.

All the countries above are great places to visit!
